Danny Tomkins is an independent local builder with nearly 30 years experience, based in Cheltenham, Gloucestershire. Danny is reliable and takes pride in the quality of his work, ensuring that your next project is completed to the highest possible standard.

With extensive experience in all types of roofing, Danny is able to provide roof repairs, fix leaking roofs, general roof maintenance and even install completely new tiled roofs, slate roofs and flat roofs.

He can also install roof lights, fascias, soffits and guttering as per the requirements of your project, in addition to repairing and maintaining conservatory roofs, shed roofs and roofs on garden outbuildings.

Danny specialises in Cotswold Stone roofing and leadwork, and has completed a number of roofing projects across Cheltenham, Gloucester and the Cotswolds. So, whether you require a tiled roof, slate roof, fibreglass roof or rubber roof weve got you covered.

Danny also provides a range of additional services from general building projects through to routine property maintenance in Cheltenham, Gloucester and the surrounding areas.

This includes masonry and brickwork, interior and exterior plastering, first and second fix carpentry, plumbing, decorating and much more.

Kitchen installation, bathroom installation and full decoration can also be provided, and Danny will be more than happy to discuss your project with you to understand your requirements and provide you with a free, no-obligation quote.

So, what are you waiting for? Get in touch with Danny today to turn your next building, renovation or roofing project into a reality.
